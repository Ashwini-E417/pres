Overview - Prestige Southern Star

Building Dreams, Creating Homes: Our Story

Prestige Southern Star is an upcoming gated community apartment project by Prestige Constructions, strategically located in Akshaya Nagar, Begur Road, Off Bannerghatta Road, South Bengaluru. This premium development offers excellent connectivity to Bannerghatta Road and Hosur Road, with proximity to metro stations ensuring seamless travel. With 80% of the land dedicated to open spaces and lush gardens, Prestige Southern Star offers a serene environment that balances urban living with nature. Residents will enjoy many modern amenities and a community designed for comfort and convenience.
